Step-by-step — coordinated by SwapMatch, executed on FIFA's system
⚠️
SwapMatch Platform Notice

SwapMatch does not process, transfer, or execute any ticket exchange. The following guide directs you to complete the transfer exclusively through FIFA's official ticketing system. All actions are your sole responsibility. Screenshots, PDFs, and barcode images are not valid — only an official FIFA transfer is.

01
Log In to FIFA Ticketing
Visit tickets.fifa.com and sign in with the account that holds your tickets. Use the credentials from your original ticket purchase.
02
Check Transfer Eligibility
Navigate to "My Tickets" and verify your ticket shows "Transfer Eligible." Guest tickets and certain categories may not qualify. Do not proceed if ineligible.
03
Initiate the Transfer
Select "Transfer Ticket" and enter the recipient's FIFA account email. Both parties must have active, verified FIFA Ticketing accounts. No transfer is possible without this.
04
Recipient Must Accept
The other party receives a FIFA email and must log in and accept the transfer within the FIFA-specified deadline. Once accepted, the ticket appears in their account only.
05
Return to SwapMatch — Mark Complete
Update your proposal status on SwapMatch to "Completed via FIFA." Leave feedback to update both Trust Scores. This closes the swap record for audit purposes.
