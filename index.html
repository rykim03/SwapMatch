Official FIFA Transfer Guide
⚠️
SwapMatch Platform Notice

SwapMatch does not process, transfer, or execute any ticket exchange. The following guide directs you to complete the transfer exclusively through FIFA's official ticketing system. All actions are your sole responsibility.

01
Log in to FIFA Ticketing
Visit tickets.fifa.com and sign in with the account that holds your tickets. Use the same credentials you used during the original ticket purchase.
02
Navigate to "My Tickets"
Find the ticket you want to transfer. Check that it shows a "Transfer Eligible" status — not all ticket types can be transferred. Guest tickets and certain categories may not qualify.
03
Initiate the Transfer
Select "Transfer Ticket" and enter the recipient's FIFA account email. Both parties must have active FIFA Ticketing accounts. The transfer cannot be completed without this.
04
Recipient Accepts
The other party receives an email from FIFA and must log in and accept the transfer within the deadline specified. Once accepted, the ticket appears in their account.
05
Mark Complete on SwapMatch
Return to SwapMatch and update your proposal status to "Completed via FIFA." Both parties can then leave feedback to update Trust Scores.
